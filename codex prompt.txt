==========================================================
PROJECT OBJECTIVE
==========================================================

The objective is NOT simply to predict heart disease.

The objective is to create a generalized Heart State Model that learns a patient's cardiovascular condition and produces:

1. Heart Risk Score (0-100)
2. Heart State Probability
3. Feature Importance
4. Latent Heart Representation

This model will later be integrated into a Digital Twin system.
